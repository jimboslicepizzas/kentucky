My model is a hybrid: 'Feed 'em and Board 'em.'

The Execution Roadmap

Phase 1: The Scout & Purchase (Months 1-3)

Goal: Secure 40+ acres of "Unrestricted" or "Agricultural" land.

Budget: $200,000 - $300,000.

  • Target: Rolling pasture for future campground, public water access.
  • Zoning Check: Confirm "Accessory Structures" allowed for Commissary Barn.
  • Subdivision Check: Ensure ability to sell 3-5 acre parcels later.

Phase 2: The "Commissary Barn" (Months 3-6)

The Strategy: Solo Entrepreneur Advantage. Living in an efficiency apartment inside the barn to reduce overhead.

The Build ($45k - $60k):

  • 40x60 Pole Barn (HQ).
  • Partitioned "Clean Room" for food prep.
  • Commercial grease trap & mop sink.

Utilities:

  • Septic: 1,500+ Gallon tank (sized for future cottages).
  • Water: Public tap required for permits.

Phase 3: The Pizza Launch (Month 6)

Goal: Immediate Cash Flow via "Statewide Mobile Food Unit" permit.

Locations:

  • Weekdays: St. Claire Regional Medical Center (Lunch Rush).
  • Weekends: Downtown Morehead "Trail Town" events.

Phase 4: The Retreat (Year 1-3)

Asset Strategy: High-Margin Passive Income.

The Product: A-Frame Cabins ($25k-$40k Kit Build).

Target Market: Cincinnati/Lexington professionals needing a digital detox.
